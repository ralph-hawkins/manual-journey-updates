Concentration ranges of the ingredients

To tell us about the concentration ranges, you'll need to upload a PDF which lists the ingredients.

What you'll need to tell us

List every ingredient in order of the largest to the smallest.

For every ingredient in the product, you'll need to tell us the:

  • International Nomenclature of Cosmetic Ingredients (INCI) name
  • Chemical Abstracts Service (CAS) number

The National Poison Information Service (NPIS) needs to know about some ingredients in the product – list these first with the exact concentrations, in order of the largest to the smallest.

For the other ingredients, list them in order of the largest concentration to the smallest.

Concentration ranges

Use these concentration ranges:

  • above 75% w/w up to 100% w/w
  • above 50% w/w up to 75% w/w
  • above 25% w/w up to 50% w/w
  • above 10% w/w up to 25% w/w
  • above 5% w/w up to 10% w/w
  • above 1% w/w up to 5% w/w
  • above 0.1% w/w up to 1% w/w
  • up to and including 0.1% w/w
