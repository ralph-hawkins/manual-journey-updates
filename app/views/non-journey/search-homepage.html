Search for cosmetic products available in the UK and notified with the Office for Product Safety and Standards (OPSS).

How to access the service

If you're using this service for the first time, send an email to opss.enquiries@beis.gov.uk to ask for an account.

Trading Standards officers

If you've used the Product Safety Database (PSD), you can use your PSD sign in details (username and password).

What you can search for

You can search for cosmetic products by:

  • product name
  • product category
  • responsible person
The ‘responsible person’ (RP) is the company (or individual) responsible for ensuring the regulations are followed. Every cosmetic product placed on the UK market must have an RP.

National Poison Information Service (NPIS) staff

You can find out the ingredients in a cosmetics product.

Trading Standards officers

You can check if the cosmetic product has been notified with OPSS.

What you can't search for

You can't search for cosmetic products taken off the UK market before 1 February 2020 but still used in the UK.

Some ingredient information and packaging images may be missing for cosmetic products notified with the EU before 1 February 2020.
